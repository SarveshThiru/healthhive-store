About HealthHive

About this app

HealthHive is a complete health companion designed to track and manage core vital signs: heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar, and blood oxygen levels. The app helps users monitor trends, receive personalized insights, and maintain long-term wellness data.

With clean visual charts, smart reminders, and privacy-focused design, HealthHive helps you take control of your health—anytime, anywhere.

Highlights

Vital Tracking
Record heart rate, blood sugar, BP & SpO₂ every day.
Trends & Insights
AI-powered analysis to identify patterns and changes.
Reminders
Custom alerts for medication, checkups, and daily logs.
Privacy First
Secure health data handling aligned with best practices.

Technical & Privacy

HealthHive is built with strict data protection standards, encrypted storage, and scalable cloud integration capability. Designed exclusively for iPhone, the app ensures smooth performance and accessibility compliance.
